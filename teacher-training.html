About the Program

Our 200-Hour Yoga Teacher Training is a comprehensive, immersive program designed to deepen your practice and prepare you to teach yoga with confidence and authenticity. Whether you aspire to teach professionally or simply want to deepen your personal practice, this program offers the perfect foundation.

Our program is Yoga Alliance certified, ensuring that upon completion, you'll be eligible for RYT-200 registration. The curriculum combines traditional yogic philosophy with modern teaching methodology, anatomy, and sequencing.

Curriculum & Syllabus

Module 1: Foundations & Philosophy

40 hours
  • History and philosophy of yoga
  • The Eight Limbs of Yoga
  • Yamas and Niyamas
  • Introduction to Sanskrit
  • Ethics for yoga teachers

Module 2: Asana Practice & Alignment

50 hours
  • Asana breakdown and alignment principles
  • Standing, seated, and supine poses
  • Inversions and arm balances
  • Modifications and variations
  • Hands-on adjustments

Module 3: Anatomy & Physiology

30 hours
  • Musculoskeletal system
  • Yoga anatomy and biomechanics
  • Injury prevention
  • Special populations considerations
  • Energy body and chakras

Module 4: Teaching Methodology

45 hours
  • Sequencing principles
  • Class planning and theme development
  • Verbal cues and instruction techniques
  • Creating safe and inclusive spaces
  • Teaching practicum

Module 5: Pranayama & Meditation

20 hours
  • Breathing techniques
  • Pranayama practice
  • Meditation methods
  • Guided relaxation
  • Integration into classes

Module 6: Business of Yoga

15 hours
  • Career paths in yoga
  • Marketing and branding
  • Insurance and legal considerations
  • Building your teaching practice
  • Continuing education opportunities
